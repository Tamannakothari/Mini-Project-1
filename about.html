Our Mission

AMAN Babson is Babson College’s South Asian cultural organization. Our mission is to celebrate South Asian traditions, music, dance, and food while creating a welcoming community for students from all backgrounds. We organize performances, workshops, festivals, and social events to connect students and share culture.

What We Do

  • Cultural showcases with dance, music, and fashion
  • Bollywood dance workshops
  • Celebration of festivals like Diwali, Holi, and Eid
  • Social and networking events for students
  • Empowering leadership and performance opportunities through the executive board
