# Client Brief

**Client:** AMAN (South Asian Student Association at Babson College) – represented the AMAN Executive Board  

**Purpose:**  
To create a central website for AMAN that showcases events, culture, leadership, and provides an easy way for students to learn about and join the organization.

**Audience:**  
Babson students (especially underclassmen and new students) who are interested in South Asian culture, dance, community, and campus involvement. Visitors need to quickly understand what AMAN is, see what we do, and find ways to get involved.

**Key action:**  
Join AMAN (through the Join page/form) or attend events.

---

# How I Identified the Need

As Co-President of AMAN, I realized through conversations with the executive board that we currently do not have a centralized website. Most of our information is scattered across Instagram and word-of-mouth.

During one of our weekly e-board meetings, this gap was brought up, and we discussed how having a website would:
- Make AMAN more accessible to new students
- Provide a permanent place for information (beyond social media)
- Better showcase our performances, events, and team

Based on this discussion, I decided to build a website for AMAN as my client project.

---

# Pages/sections needed:

- Home (introduction + highlight video of AMAN show)
- About (mission and what AMAN does)
- Leadership (Spring 2026 executive board)
- Countries (South Asian cultures represented)
- Gallery (Fall 2025 show moments)
- Join AMAN (form for new members)

# Style preferences:

From discussion with the e-board:
- Bright, vibrant colors reflecting South Asian culture  
- Fun but clean design (not too cluttered)  
- Visual emphasis on dance, performance, and community  
- Easy navigation and mobile-friendly layout

# Summary

The goal of this website is to create a **central digital presence for AMAN** that improves visibility, accessibility, and engagement. It should feel welcoming, vibrant, and reflective of the culture and energy of the organization while also being easy to navigate and informative.
